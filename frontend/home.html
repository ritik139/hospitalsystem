Powerful Features Built for Clinical Teams

From patient intake to billing — every workflow is covered with an intuitive, role-based interface.

Patient Management

Register, track, and manage patient records in real-time with full history, conditions, and appointment logs.

Smart Scheduling

Book, reschedule, and track appointments with one click. Auto-assign doctors based on specialty and availability.

Billing & Invoicing
